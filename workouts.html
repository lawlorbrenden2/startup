Grip a barbell with hands slightly wider than shoulder width. The bar should be directly over the shoulders. A void arching your back.Slowly lift the bar or dumbbells off the rack, if using. Lower the bar to the chest, about nipple level, allowing elbows to bend out to the side, about 45 degrees away from the body.Stop lowering when your elbows are just below the bench." Press feet into the floor as you push the bar back up to return to starting position."
